Our Services


  1. 1.Virtual Interior Design Consultations

    With just a few clicks, connect with our expert designers for virtual consultations that cater to your unique space and preferences. Whether you're working with a small apartment or a large home, we provide customized design plans tailored to your budget and style.

  2. 2.In-Person Design Consultations

    Prefer face-to-face guidance? Visit our showroom in Addis Ababa to meet with our designers in person. Get hands-on with materials, and view design samples to create your perfect space.

  3. 3.Augmented Reality (AR) Design Visualization

    Experience your design in a whole new way with our AR tool. Simply upload photos of your space, and visualize how our recommended furniture and decor will look. It's the most immersive and accurate way to plan your room layout.

  4. 4.Custom Design Packages

    We offer a variety of design packages, each featuring carefully curated design elements that fit your style and budget. From small tweaks to full overhauls, we ensure every design solution meets your expectations without breaking the bank.

  5. 5.Shopping Suggestions & Vendor Partnerships

    Our experts recommend affordable and local decor options, sourcing items from popular stores and unique local brands. We collaborate with trusted furniture shops to ensure you get the best prices and quality for your purchases.
