Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? Find answers below!

How do virtual consultations work?

Our virtual consultations are conducted through a secure video call. During the session, we discuss your design ideas and explore possible solutions using AR tools. It's quick, convenient, and easy!

What is Augmented Reality (AR) and how does it help?

AR allows us to create interactive visualizations of your space in real-time. You can see how different designs would look in your space before making any changes-no more guesswork!

What is the pricing structure for your services?

Pricing varies depending on the scope of the project. We offer competitive rates and will give you a clear estimate during our consultation.

How do I schedule a consultation?

Simply head to our Contact page and fill out the form to schedule your consultation. We'll get back to you within 24 hours!
